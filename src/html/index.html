Who We Are

We are Androsaxons; Professional IT enthusiasts with expertise in a wide variety of business domains.

Some of the businesses we helped operates in industries such as:

  • Public Sector

    Working closely with our clients in public sector we help transform their IT operations to catch up with latest technology. We built software platforms with modern web technologies and cloud based infrastructure for clients working in and regulating health, statistics, trade and investment industries.

  • Tel-co

    With the privilige of having worked with some of the biggest telephone companies with millions of subscribers we built up immense expertise in tel-co. We are here to improve the way you handle your data; We can make it easier for you to process, analyse, and secure sensitive end-user data without comprimising agility of your business.

  • Banking

    Banking with always flowing transaction traffic or sheer size of data to keep an eye on and make sense of, we have tackled it and conquered it. We are happy to do so again to help you, always up for a challenge!
